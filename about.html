About Us

Quality products, factory-direct.

We help overseas buyers source reliable elderly care equipment from verified Chinese manufacturers — without the guesswork, without the middlemen, and without compromising on quality.

What we do

CaringTech is a B2B sourcing partner for elderly care products. We work directly with specialized manufacturers in China's hardware and medical equipment clusters — factories that produce grab bars, walking canes, patient transfer chairs, and related products for export markets worldwide.

Our role is quality assurance and supply chain management. We vet factories, verify product quality through on-site inspection, manage export documentation, and ensure consistent standards across every order — from a sample batch of 10 units to a container of 10,000.

We don't claim certifications we don't have. What we offer is transparency: real factory data (review scores, on-time rates, repeat buyer percentages), real product specifications, and direct communication with the production team behind every product.

Why buyers work with us

Verified factories, not traders

Every supplier on our platform has a track record on China's largest B2B marketplace — years of history, thousands of reviews, verifiable on-time shipment rates. No anonymous trading companies.

What you see is what you get

Every specification on our product pages comes from the factory's actual listing data — materials, dimensions, weight ratings, color options. No embellishment, no fake certifications.

Your sourcing partner on the ground

We handle supplier communication, quality sampling, export packaging, and logistics coordination. You get the products you ordered, on time, at the price you expected.
