About Smart Elderly Care

Trusted global OEM/ODM partner for elderly assistive products since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2014 in Shenzhen, China, Smart Elderly Care began with a simple mission: make aging safer and more dignified through innovative assistive products. What started as a small R&D team has grown into a leading manufacturer with a 15,000 m² ISO 13485 certified facility.

Today we export to over 50 countries and serve more than 200 B2B partners including nursing home chains, medical distributors, government healthcare programs, and home care agencies. Our products are trusted by healthcare professionals worldwide.

We invest 8% of annual revenue in R&D, continuously improving our products based on feedback from caregivers, occupational therapists, and end-users. Every product we ship meets rigorous international quality standards.
