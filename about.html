About Us

We make products that care

CaringTech is a manufacturer of elderly care products, not a reseller. We design, engineer, and build every product in-house — because when it comes to resident safety, there is no room for compromise.

Our story

CaringTech was founded in 2011 by a team of biomedical engineers and care facility operators who saw a gap in the market: most elderly care equipment was either consumer-grade (not durable enough for commercial use) or hospital-grade (prohibitively expensive for nursing homes and assisted living facilities).

We set out to build a different kind of company — one that manufactures professional-grade equipment at accessible price points by selling direct to facilities, with no distributor markups. Fifteen years later, our products are used in over 500 care facilities across North America and Europe.

Everything we make is designed, tested, and assembled in our ISO 13485 certified facility. We control the entire process — from raw materials to final inspection — because that is the only way to guarantee the quality and reliability that care environments demand.

Our Mission

To make professional-grade elderly care equipment accessible to every care facility, regardless of size or budget, by manufacturing direct and eliminating unnecessary costs.

Our Vision

A world where no care facility has to compromise on safety equipment due to cost, and where every resident lives with dignity supported by reliable, well-designed tools.

Our Values

Quality without compromise. Direct relationships with our customers. Continuous improvement driven by feedback from the caregivers who use our products every day.

In-house manufacturing

Our 45,000 sq ft facility houses R&D, production, and quality assurance under one roof. By controlling the entire manufacturing process, we maintain consistent quality while keeping costs competitive.

Every product undergoes a 27-point inspection before it leaves our facility. We test weight loads to 1.5x the rated capacity, cycle mechanical components 10,000 times in endurance testing, and validate all electronic systems against medical device standards.

When you buy from CaringTech, you buy directly from the manufacturer — no distributors, no importers, no middlemen. That means faster lead times, better pricing, and a direct line to the engineering team if you ever need support.
