Patient Transfer Aid

Safe Lifting • Ergonomic Design • Portable & Foldable

The Patient Transfer Aid is designed to safely move elderly or disabled individuals between beds, wheelchairs, and commodes with minimal strain on caregivers. Its ergonomic frame and padded support system ensure dignity and comfort for the patient while reducing the risk of caregiver injury. Ideal for nursing homes, hospitals, and home care.
