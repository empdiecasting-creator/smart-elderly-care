Smart Support Rail

LED Ambient Lighting • Anti-Slip Grip • Easy Wall Mount

The Smart Support Rail offers safety and comfort for seniors in bathrooms, hallways, and bedrooms. With a built-in LED strip that gently lights the path during nighttime and an ergonomic anti-slip surface, it prevents falls in the most vulnerable areas of the home. Ideal for nursing homes, assisted living facilities, and home care environments.
