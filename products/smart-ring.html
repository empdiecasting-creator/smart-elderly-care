Health Smart Ring

Continuous Health Monitoring • Caregiver Dashboard • Medical Grade Sensors

The Health Smart Ring is a discreet wearable device that continuously tracks heart rate, blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂), sleep quality, and daily activity. Designed specifically for the elderly, it connects to a caregiver dashboard for real-time health alerts. Lightweight, waterproof, and comfortable for 24/7 wear.
