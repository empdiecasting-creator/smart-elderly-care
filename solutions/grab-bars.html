Features that matter for commercial use

Both product lines share these essential qualities for professional care environments.

Stainless Steel Core

Rust-resistant 201 or 304 stainless steel. Built for the constant humidity of commercial bathrooms. 304 grade adds molybdenum for superior corrosion resistance against chlorine-based cleaning agents.

Proven Anti-Slip Surface

Two grip technologies, same result: secure hold with wet or soapy hands. The knurled line uses a permanent machined diamond pattern. The coated line uses a full-length textured powder finish — softer and warmer to the touch.

Universal Mounting

Standard flange and screw mounting system compatible with drywall (with backing), tile, concrete, and fiberglass surfaces. Straight bar design keeps installation simple — no complex angles to align.

Multiple Finish Options

Match any facility interior. Premium line offers polished bright (classic), matte black (modern), or gold electroplate (warm/luxury). Economy line offers warm yellow or clean white powder coat.

Full Size Range

From 30 cm toilet-area bars to 80 cm full-shower rails. Order exactly the lengths you need — no forced assortment. Both lines cover the most commonly requested sizes for nursing home and hospital bathrooms.

Bulk-Ready Packaging

Each unit individually wrapped to prevent scratches during transit. Bulk orders palletized by size and finish. Both supplying factories ship to Amazon FBA, Wish, and independent e-commerce sellers — they understand export logistics.
