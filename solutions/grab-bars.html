Key features

Designed for the relentless demands of commercial bathrooms, hallways, and patient rooms.

Anti-Microbial Coating

Silver-ion infused surface eliminates 99.9% of bacteria within 2 hours. Tested to ISO 22196 standards — critical for infection control in healthcare settings.

500 lb Weight Capacity

Tested and certified to support up to 500 lbs (227 kg) static load. Exceeds ADA requirements by 100% — built for bariatric and high-acuity applications.

Quick-Install Mounting

Patented 3-point mounting system installs in under 15 minutes per unit. Compatible with drywall, tile, concrete, and fiberglass surfaces. All hardware included.

Corrosion-Resistant

316 stainless steel core with powder-coated finish. Rated for continuous high-humidity environments. Passes 1,000-hour salt spray test (ASTM B117).

Textured Anti-Slip Grip

PEBAX elastomer grip surface maintains friction even when wet or soapy. 1.25" and 1.5" diameter options available for different hand sizes and grip strengths.

Configurable Design

Available in 12", 18", 24", 32", 36", and 48" lengths. Straight, L-shaped, and angled configurations. Six standard finishes to match any facility interior.
