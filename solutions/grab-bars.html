Build Quality

The grip is part of the metal

Unlike grab bars with rubber sleeves or spray-on coatings, our knurled texture is machined directly into 32mm 304 stainless tubing. It cannot peel, crack, or wear smooth. Soap, water, cleaning chemicals — the grip stays the same.

Permanent anti-slip grip

Diamond knurl pattern machined into the tube. Secure grip with wet or soapy hands — no sleeve to replace, ever.

304 stainless steel

Solid 32mm tube resists rust and corrosion far better than chrome-plated or 201-grade alternatives. Suitable for humid bathroom environments.

50 kg rated load

Tested to support 50 kg static load when properly mounted into wall studs or backing plates. Suitable for daily assisted use.

Three finishes available

Brushed, matte black, or brushed gold. All on the same 304 stainless tube. Match any bathroom interior.

Three finishes, same 304 steel core

Every finish is built on the same 32mm 304 stainless tube. Pick the look that fits your customer's interior.
