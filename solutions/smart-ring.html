Smart Ring

Key features

Designed for seniors who resist wearing medical devices — the ring form factor drives dramatically higher compliance rates.

Continuous Vitals Monitoring

Tracks heart rate (every 5 minutes), blood oxygen (SpO2), skin temperature, and heart rate variability. Early warning alerts when vitals deviate from each resident's baseline.

Fall Detection

Built-in 6-axis IMU detects fall events with 98% sensitivity. Alerts are sent to the nursing dashboard and designated smartphones within seconds — no button press required.

Sleep Quality Tracking

Measures sleep duration, stages (light, deep, REM), and nocturnal movement. Staff can identify residents developing sleep disturbances — often an early indicator of declining health.

IP68 Waterproof

Fully submersible to 1.5 meters for 30 minutes. Residents never need to remove it — showering, hand washing, and bathing are all fine. This continuous-wear capability is what makes the data reliable.

5-Day Battery Life

Charges fully in 90 minutes via USB-C magnetic charging dock. Bulk charging stations available — charge 20 rings simultaneously on one power outlet.

Facility Dashboard

Web-based dashboard shows real-time vitals for every resident. Sort by alert status, view trends over time, and export reports for regulatory compliance or family updates.

Application scenarios

The ring form factor opens up health monitoring to residents who refuse traditional medical alert devices.

Nursing Homes

Population health at a glance

The facility dashboard gives nurses a single-screen view of every resident's current status. Color-coded alerts prioritize who needs attention. Spot trends — a resident whose sleep quality declines over three nights may be developing a UTI or respiratory issue before symptoms appear.

Assisted Living

Independence with oversight

Residents in assisted living value their independence. The ring provides safety monitoring without visible medical equipment. Family members can receive selected data via a companion app — giving them peace of mind without daily check-in calls.

Memory Care

Wander management & safety net

For residents with dementia who cannot reliably use call buttons, the ring provides passive safety. Fall detection works automatically. Location tracking via facility gateways helps staff locate wandering residents quickly.
