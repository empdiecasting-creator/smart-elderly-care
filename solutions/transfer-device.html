Transfer Device

A safe, dignified patient transfer solution that protects both residents and caregivers. Smooth electric lift with one-caregiver operation — designed to reduce workplace injuries without compromising resident comfort.

Key features

Every element is engineered to reduce physical strain on caregivers while ensuring resident dignity.

Smooth Electric Lift

Whisper-quiet electric actuator raises and lowers residents smoothly — no jerky hydraulic movements. Variable speed control with soft-start and soft-stop for resident comfort.

True One-Caregiver Operation

Unlike traditional lifts requiring two staff members, our device is operated safely by a single caregiver. Intuitive controls reduce training time to under 30 minutes.

350 lb Weight Capacity

Heavy-duty steel frame with reinforced stress points. Tested to 1.5x rated capacity. Suitable for bariatric applications without requiring a separate device.

Compact & Foldable

Folds flat to 8 inches for storage against a wall or in a closet. Weighs 65 lbs with integrated transport wheels — easily moved between rooms by one person.

Comfort-First Sling Design

Four-point padded sling distributes weight evenly. Breathable, antimicrobial fabric. Available in three sizes (S/M/L) with color-coded loops for quick identification.

Long-Life Battery

Lithium-ion battery handles 80+ transfers on a single charge. Hot-swappable design — keep a spare charged and never experience downtime. Full recharge in 3 hours.

Application scenarios

From acute care to long-term residence, the Transfer Device adapts to your workflow.

Nursing Homes

Daily care routines

One caregiver can manage bed-to-chair, chair-to-bathroom, and floor-to-bed transfers throughout the day. The compact folded size means each wing can have its own device without storage issues.

Hospitals

Post-surgery & rehabilitation

Smooth, controlled movement is critical for post-operative patients. The soft-start/soft-stop actuator and padded sling minimize discomfort during transfers from bed to physiotherapy.

Home Care

Family caregiver safety

Family members without professional training can operate the device safely after a brief orientation. Prevents the back injuries that plague family caregivers — the leading reason they seek outside help.
