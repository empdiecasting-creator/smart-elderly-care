Key features

Every feature is designed with both resident safety and caregiver workflow in mind.

Automatic Fall Detection

Built-in accelerometer and gyroscope detect falls instantly. Alerts are sent to nursing stations within seconds, reducing response time by up to 60%.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Facility-wide location tracking with geofencing capabilities. Know where every resident is — critical for memory care units and large campuses.

7-Day Battery Life

Long-lasting battery minimizes charging interruptions. USB-C rechargeable, full charge in under 2 hours. Low-battery alerts notify staff in advance.

Ergonomic Grip Design

Soft-touch, contoured handle reduces hand fatigue. Non-slip surface tested under wet conditions. Adjustable height accommodates residents of all statures.

Waterproof (IPX5)

Withstands rigorous daily cleaning protocols in hospital environments. Resistant to water jets from any direction — safe for shower room use.

Lightweight Aluminum Body

Aircraft-grade aluminum alloy construction. Weighs only 1.2 lbs (540 g) yet supports up to 300 lbs. Corrosion-resistant finish in matte silver or dark bronze.
