# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Overseas B2B buyers — nursing home procurement managers, hospital facility directors, assisted living operators. They need to equip their facilities with elderly care products at competitive bulk pricing. They value transparency (real specs, real supplier data) over marketing fluff.

## Product Purpose

A sourcing platform that connects overseas buyers with verified Chinese manufacturers of elderly care equipment. Four product lines: walking canes, grab bars, patient transfer chairs, and health monitoring rings. Every product page is backed by real factory data — no fake certifications, no made-up specs.

## Brand Personality

Straightforward, trustworthy, no-BS. Not a fancy med-tech startup. Not a generic AliExpress dropshipper. A sourcing partner who does the homework so buyers don't have to.

## Anti-references

- Fake certifications (ISO 13485, CE, FDA — don't claim what you don't have)
- Buzzword-loaded SaaS landing pages (streamline, empower, cutting-edge)
- Dropshipping language (no "1-piece price", "铺货到Amazon")
- Over-designed AI aesthetic (glassmorphism, gradient text, cream backgrounds)

## Design Principles

- Show, don't tell — real product images, real specs, real supplier data
- Honest over polished — transparency builds more trust than perfection
- Factory-direct, not middleman — every page reinforces direct-from-manufacturer
- Practical over flashy — buyers are making procurement decisions, not browsing Dribbble

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. All images need alt text. Mobile-first — procurement managers browse on phones too. No motion without reduced-motion fallback.
